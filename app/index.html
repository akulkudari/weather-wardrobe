Who are we?

We are Akul Kudari, and Sherif Elfiky, the team behind the interface you see on your screen. We are two Computer Engineering student at UC San Diego. We created this application to track the weather, and to take the difficulty out of deciding what to wear, rain or shine

How does it work?

Using our weather API, and various temperature and humidity sensor data, we observe the weather conditions in your area. Then using our state of the art LLM, we ask what the best option would be to wear to keep you both stylish, and protected from the elements. We can access weather data from all over the world to bring you the best closet choices, no matter where you are.
